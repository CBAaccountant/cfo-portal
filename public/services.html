What Drives Your Price
📈
Transaction Volume
More transactions = more work to categorize, reconcile, and review. This is the single biggest pricing factor. Under 100 transactions/mo is simple; 300+ requires dedicated attention.
🏗
Industry Complexity
Construction (job costing), e-commerce (inventory, multi-channel), and manufacturing have more complex books than a consulting firm. Complex industries command a 20-40% premium.
👥
Payroll & Headcount
Businesses with employees need payroll reconciliation, benefits tracking, and 1099/W-2 compliance. Each layer of HR complexity adds to the monthly scope.
📚
Catch-Up & Cleanup
Behind on your books? Catch-up bookkeeping is typically billed as a one-time project ($500 - $5,000+) depending on how many months need reconciling.
